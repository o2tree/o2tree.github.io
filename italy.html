Cinque Terre is not very far from Florence, between these five fabulous villages –
*  Monterosso al Mare,
*  Vernazza,
*  Corniglia,
*  Manarola and
*  Riomaggiore 
– and the Cradle of the Renaissance there are only about 170 km! So, if you are visiting Florence and Cinque Terre is on your wish list,
 you can easily take a day trip and enjoy this breathtaking coastal landscape.

The most comfortable way to get around Cinque Terre is surely by the 5 Terre Express train: this train connects all five villages, burrowing through tunnels along the cliffs. You can buy the 5 Terre Express day pass in La Spezia and make a stop at every village.

Going from Florence to La Spezia
---
By train – the journey lasts approximately 2 hours and a half. From La Spezia railway station, take the 5 Terre Express train or go to the seafront in order to take the ferry boat.
