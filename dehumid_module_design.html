Peltier vs Desiccant — comparison

Property Peltier (TEC) Desiccant wheel
Moisture removal Condensation (air cooled below dew point) Adsorption (chemical affinity to water)
Best RH range RH > 50% (high humidity) RH 20–50% (low humidity)
Temperature sensitivity Less effective below ~15°C Works well even at 5°C
Power consumption Low–medium (12–60W) Medium (regen heater needed, 30–100W)
Moving parts None (solid state) Slow-rotating wheel + motor
Noise Very quiet Low (wheel motor)
Maintenance Drain condensate only Periodic desiccant check / replacement
Best for enclosure dryer Small enclosures, moderate humidity, low noise priority Large enclosures, very dry target, cold environments
