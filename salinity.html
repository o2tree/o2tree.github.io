# The Conway medium is a classic and widely used fertilizer formulation for growing marine microalgae in aquaculture hatcheries. It provides a balanced mix of major nutrients (Nitrogen, Phosphorus) and essential trace metals and vitamins. Unlike a single product, "Conway fertilizer" refers to a recipe. You can either purchase it as a pre-made kit or mix it yourself from laboratory-grade chemicals. --- ### What is Conway Medium? It is a defined, chemical solution used to enrich seawater to create the perfect "soup" for growing specific species of microalgae (e.g., *Isochrysis*, *Chaetoceros*, *Tetraselmis*). It was developed by the renowned algal physiologist W. A. P. Conway. ### Composition of Conway Medium The classic recipe is divided into three separate stock solutions to prevent chemicals from reacting with each other and precipitating during storage. --- #### Solution A (Macronutrients) * Function: Provides the main building blocks for algal growth (Nitrogen, Phosphorus). * Typical Composition (per 1 liter of fresh water): * Ammonium Nitrate (NH‚ÇÑNO‚ÇÉ) or Sodium Nitrate (NaNO‚ÇÉ): 50 - 100 g * Sodium Dihydrogen Phosphate (NaH‚ÇÇPO‚ÇÑ ¬∑ 2H‚ÇÇO): 5 - 10 g * Dosage: 1 mL of Solution A is added per 1 liter of algae culture. ---- #### Solution B (Trace Metals + Iron) * Function: Provides essential micronutrients, including chelated iron. * Typical Composition (per 1 liter of fresh water): * Iron Chelate (Fe-EDTA or Fe-Citrate): 5 - 10 g (This is your bioavailable iron source) * Manganese Chloride (MnCl‚ÇÇ ¬∑ 4H‚ÇÇO): 0.5 g * Boric Acid (H‚ÇÉBO‚ÇÉ): 5 g * Zinc Sulfate (ZnSO‚ÇÑ ¬∑ 7H‚ÇÇO): 0.5 g * Cobalt Nitrate (Co(NO‚ÇÉ)‚ÇÇ ¬∑ 6H‚ÇÇO): 0.1 g * Copper Sulfate (CuSO‚ÇÑ ¬∑ 5H‚ÇÇO): 0.02 g * Sodium Molybdate (Na‚ÇÇMoO‚ÇÑ ¬∑ 2H‚ÇÇO): 0.1 g * Dosage: 1 mL of Solution B is added per 1 liter of algae culture. --- #### Solution C (Vitamins) * Function: Provides essential vitamins that many algae cannot synthesize themselves. * Typical Composition (per 1 liter of fresh water): * Vitamin B12 (Cyanocobalamin): 0.1 g * Vitamin B1 (Thiamine HCl): 1.0 g * Biotin: 0.01 g * Important: This solution is highly perishable. Prepare Stock Solutions: Dissolve the chemicals for Solutions A, B, and C in separate 1-liter bottles of distilled or RO water. 2.
